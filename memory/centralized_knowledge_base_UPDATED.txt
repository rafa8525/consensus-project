Centralized Knowledge Base

Purpose: Enable AI agents to store and share insights effectively

Benefits:
- Facilitate information sharing and collaboration
- Enhance overall system intelligence and efficiency

Implementation:
- Develop and implement a centralized knowledge base
- Ensure seamless access and retrieval of information
- Foster collaboration and continuous learning among AI agents
