Testing Plan for Automatic VPN Activation Feature:

1. Load Testing: Evaluate performance under anticipated loads.
2. Stress Testing: Assess stability and responsiveness in extreme conditions.
3. Concurrency Testing: Handle multiple simultaneous user interactions.
4. Endurance Testing: Validate sustained performance over time.
5. Failover Testing: Ensure seamless transition to backup systems during failures.
6. Considerations: Security, scalability, network latency, and compatibility.
